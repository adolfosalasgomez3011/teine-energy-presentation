Company overview

  • Teine Energy is a provider of oil and gas exploration and development services based in Calgary, Canada
  • The company focuses on the acquisition, exploration, production and development of oil and gas assets in Saskatchewan, Western Canada
Operational Focus
  • Teine Energy operates in various regions, with a significant presence in southwestern Saskatchewan; Notably, they hold the distinction of being the Viking's largest producer in this region
Partnership
  • Teine Energy has strategically partnered with Canadian Pension Plan Investments (CPP Investments), enhancing their capabilities and reach
  • In 2010, Teine Energy issued 76$M in convertible debt to CPP Investments
  • In 2017, CPP invested a further 885$M to enable Teine Energy to purchase Penn West Petroleum's Saskatchewan assets
  • CPP holds a 90% stake in Teine Energy as a result of these investments
