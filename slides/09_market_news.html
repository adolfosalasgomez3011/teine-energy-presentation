Teine Energy looks to sell certain non-core oil and natural gas assets
March 2024

Teine is looking to unload these assets to focus operations on its core areas. These assets 2023 average daily production was 117 barrels of oil equivalent per day, yielding 2.55M net operating income. The net value of this asset is deemed 3.3$M.

Teine energy drilling activity, in year prior to April 2023
April 2023

Teine Energy has spudded a total of 295 wells in the past year. While the Viking formation remains their primary focus, they've also expanded into other areas. Notably, they spudded 6 wells in the Chauvin South field in March 2023, leveraging rights acquired from Repsol. Additionally, 5 wells in their Duvernay assets hint at more drilling activity ahead. Overall, Teine seems to be diversifying its exploration efforts beyond the Viking play.

Teine Energy purchases Alberta oil assets from Repsol, for 304$M
September 2022

Included in the deal is 95,000 net acres of oil and gas producing land, and supporting midstream infrastructure, including a network of about 1,800 kilometers of pipeline. Repsol's production from the field ranged around 6,800 barrels of oil equivalent per day.

Appointment of new COO
November 2017

Teine energy has announced the appointment of Paul Ringrose to the role of Senior Vice-President and Chief Operating Officer. Paul Ringrose has over 20 year of industry experience, where he has held various operational and management positions.

Teine Energy purchases all of Penn West Petroleum's Saskatchewan assets, for 975$M
June 2016

This deal became available so Penn West could comply with its debt obligations. Teine acquired a scalable position within one of North America's most economic light oil plays. The acquisition also adds to Teine's extensive infrastructure growing their owned and operating oil treating capacity as well and their water treating and injection capability.
